Privacy Policy

Only Care - Emotional Support Platform

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

Welcome to Only Care, operated by Innovfix Private Limited. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our emotional support platform.

Important: By using Only Care, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, display name, languages spoken
  • Creator Information: For creators, we collect additional verification documents including government-issued ID, bank account details for withdrawals
  • Payment Information: Transaction history, coin purchases, earnings records (payment processing is handled by third-party providers)

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Session data (session duration, session quality, participants, timestamps)
  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)
  • Location data (approximate location based on IP address)
  • App usage patterns and preferences

1.3 Communication Data

  • Chat messages between users
  • Customer support communications
  • Feedback and reviews
  • Reports and complaints submitted by users

1.4 Financial Data

  • Coin purchase history and transaction records
  • Wallet balance and earnings (for creators)
  • Withdrawal requests and bank account information
  • Referral earnings and commissions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Maintain Our Services

  • Enable emotional support sessions between users and support providers
  • Process coin purchases and manage virtual currency
  • Facilitate payments to support providers for their services
  • Match users with appropriate support providers based on preferences
  • Maintain and improve platform performance

2.2 To Manage Your Account

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Verify creator identities through KYC (Know Your Customer) process
  • Process withdrawal requests for creators
  • Handle subscription and membership features

2.3 For Communication

  • Send you notifications about sessions, messages, and account activity
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send promotional offers and marketing communications (you can opt-out)
  • Notify you about changes to our services or policies

2.4 For Safety and Security

  • Monitor for fraudulent or suspicious activity
  • Investigate and prevent violations of our terms of service
  • Enforce our policies and protect user safety
  • Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
  • Verify user age (minimum 18 years) and prevent underage access

2.5 For Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Conduct research and development for new features
  • Generate statistical reports and business insights
  • Optimize platform performance and call quality

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, photo, bio) is visible to other users when they browse support providers
  • Session history and duration may be visible to participants
  • Reviews and ratings you provide may be visible to other users

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf:

  • Payment Processors: To handle coin purchases and support provider payouts
  • Cloud Hosting: To store data and host our platform
  • Communication Services: To enable real-time support sessions
  • Analytics Providers: To understand usage patterns (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Customer Support Tools: To provide assistance to users
  • Marketing Platforms: To deliver promotional content

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
  • Government or law enforcement requests
  • Investigations of potential violations of our terms
  • Situations involving potential threats to safety

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication options for account security
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal information
Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Users should be mindful of the information they share publicly on their profiles.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • Access your account information and transaction history

5.2 Correction and Update

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Modify your communication preferences

5.3 Deletion

  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Note: Some information may be retained for legal or business purposes
  • Backup copies may persist for a limited time

5.4 Restriction and Objection

  • Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Restrict certain uses of your information

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience:

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality (login, security)
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous usage statistics
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Track effectiveness of advertisements

6.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (not recommended)
  • Clear cookies when closing the browser

7. Age Restrictions

Only Care is intended for users 18 years and older.

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain financial records (typically 7 years for tax purposes)

After account deletion, some information may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days, and certain records may be kept longer for legal compliance.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Support Provider-Specific Terms

11.1 Additional Data Collection for Support Providers

If you register as a support provider, we collect additional information:

  • Government-issued identification documents for verification
  • Bank account details for payment processing
  • Tax identification information (as required by law)
  • Detailed performance metrics and earnings data

11.2 Support Provider Visibility

As a support provider, your profile and certain performance statistics may be more visible to users browsing the platform, including:

  • Session rates and availability status
  • User ratings and reviews
  • Total sessions completed and response time
  • Languages spoken and areas of expertise

12. Payment and Financial Information

We use third-party payment processors to handle financial transactions. We do not store complete credit card information on our servers. Payment processors have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

We maintain records of:

  • Coin purchase history
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps
  • Withdrawal requests and processing status
  • Referral earnings and commissions

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
