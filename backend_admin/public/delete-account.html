How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Only Care account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1

Send Deletion Request

Email us at onlycare@innovfix.in from your registered email address

2

Include Required Information

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered email address or phone number
  • Your display name (if different from email)
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Confirmation that you understand deletion is permanent
3

Verification

We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate. You may be asked to provide additional verification.

4

Confirmation

Once verified, we will send you a confirmation email. Your account will be deleted within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

Data That Will Be Permanently Deleted:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, profile photo
  • Account Data: Login credentials, preferences, settings
  • Session History: Records of support sessions and conversations
  • Messages: All chat messages and communications
  • Reviews & Ratings: Any reviews you've given or received
  • Unused Coins: Any remaining coin balance (non-refundable)
  • For Support Providers: Unpaid earnings below minimum withdrawal amount

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Legal Compliance: Transaction records for tax and legal purposes (up to 7 years)
  • Financial Records: Payment history for accounting (as required by law)
  • Security Logs: IP addresses and security-related data (up to 90 days)
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated analytics data (cannot identify you)
